Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Golf Scorecard ("the App") is a mobile application for tracking golf scores, developed by DNTSK. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy describes what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores all your golf data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server. The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Golf round data (dates, scores, hole pars)
  • Player names
  • Course names
  • App preferences (language setting)

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. Deleting the App will permanently remove all locally stored data.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the App requests your explicit permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before allowing ad networks to access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies.

You can change your tracking preference at any time: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

If you deny tracking, ads will still be shown but they will not be personalized based on your activity in other apps.

GDPR (European Economic Area, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to access — request what data is held about you
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your data
  • Right to object — object to data processing for advertising
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw previously given consent at any time

The App uses Appodeal's built-in Consent Management Platform (Stack Consent Manager) to collect and manage your consent for data processing in GDPR regions. A consent dialog will be shown on first use if required by your region.

To exercise your rights regarding data collected by ad networks, please contact the respective ad network directly using the links above, or contact us and we will assist you.

CCPA (California, USA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information

The App itself does not sell personal information. However, the use of advertising identifiers by ad networks may constitute a "sale" under CCPA. You may opt out by denying tracking permission when prompted, or via device settings.

Data Retention

Local data on your device is retained until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data collected by third-party ad networks is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the ad networks, please contact us.
